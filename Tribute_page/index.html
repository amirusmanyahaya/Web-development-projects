Nelson Mandela Tribute Page

Born Rolihlahla Mandela, into the Madiba clan in the village of Mvezo, in the Eastern Cape, on 18 July 1918

In 1925, Rolihlahla Mandela attended primary school near Qunu where he was named Nelson by his teacher

In 1930, when he was 12 years old, young Rolihlahla lost his father

1934, Nelson Mandela undergoes initiation; Attends Clarkebury Boarding Institute in Engcobo

In 1937, he attended Healdtown, the Wesleyan College at Fort Beaufort

In 1942, Nelson Mandela completes a BA through the University of South Africa

Also in 1942, he began to attend the meetings of African National Congress(ANC)

In 1943, Nelson Mandela graduated with BA from Fort Hare; Enrols for an LLB at Wits University

1944, Co-founds the ANC Youth League (ANCYL); marries Evelyn Ntoko Mase – they have four children: Thembekile (1945); Makaziwe (1947 – who dies after nine months); Makgatho (1950); Makaziwe (1954)
